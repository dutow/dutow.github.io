on the name

Mental static feels like a steady buzz behind your eyes.

Thoughts drifting and weaving, returning a little brighter, a little stranger; a restless, sparkling flow pulling you from one idea to the next, where the chaos doesn’t overwhelm, it hums—alive, playful, your mind chasing small flashes of something that might just turn into magic.

// note: the name is just a Neon Genesis Evangelion reference that has stuck with me since the early 2000s. TV/radio interference, the mixing of channels, the way we tune in from one to another — a good metaphor for how I think and jump from one topic to another, even when they seem unrelated from the outside. Nowadays the term is unfortunately used to describe stress or mental fatigue, but I stick with my positive definition — this is my superpower!
